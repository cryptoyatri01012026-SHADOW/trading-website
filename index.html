Our Story

NOT A GYM.
A MOVEMENT.

Eternal Zone was built on one belief: that every person carries untapped potential waiting to be unleashed. Since 2016, we have been Kolkata's most committed training ground — where beginners become athletes and athletes become legends.

Our 15,000 sq ft facility is equipped with Olympic-grade equipment, expert coaching, and a community that pushes you harder than you push yourself.
